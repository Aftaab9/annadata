Select Leaf modeToggle from Produce on the Inspect screen

Run inferenceTFLite EfficientNetB0 runs in-browser — no server round-trip

05 · Leaf outcome

Three leaf classes — what the model returns

Healthy

HEALTHY

No actionable disease — continue normal schedule; low yield risk

Surface

SURFACE_DEFECT

Cosmetic or early stress — monitor, adjust handling, optional fungicide scout

Disease

BLIGHT_MOLD

Active blight/mold — treatment advice shown; feeds higher defect rate into Yield

06 · Leaf → Yield link

Treatment advice + optional yield handoff

A BLIGHT_MOLD or SURFACE_DEFECT result maps to a disease-risk defect rate that auto-fills the Yield simulator — no re-typing by the operator.

Leaf CV→ Treatment tips→ Defect %→ Yield sim

Leaf hold-out: 97.4% · EfficientNetB0 (5 architectures compared)

07 · Produce quality

Step 1 — Upload a produce photo

Seven process parameters — moisture, RPM, temperature, grade, defect rate, batch size, line speed — drive predicted yield % and throughput kg/h.

Market listing with Grade Card

15 · Advisory

Crop recommendation — 6 Annadata SKUs

Enter soil N/P/K, pH, rainfall, temperature — random forest recommends the best crop among six local SKUs, exported to ONNX for in-browser inference.

Hold-out accuracy · RF → ONNX
